Nosa Ojo Charles

Wishing to develop my passion for Art, I chose to move to the UK from Spain in order to be able to attend a more creative course at school.

In 2016 I graduated from the "Florence Academy of Art" where I completed a three year degree in academic drawing and painting.

Building on the techniques I learned there, I have worked to develop my own style, striving to encompass the basic tenets of academic work and classic painters. I attempt to translate their understanding of anatomy, composition and light using my own approach.

As a self-taught photographer, I explore the connection between photography and paintings. I create paintings from my own photographs, yet there are also instances where I believe that a photograph cannot be improved and should be left as such.

I currently live in Spain and continue to develop my work whilst I collaborate with The Big Art Studio.

'I am certain that creating is a form that you can accomplish when what you have imagined goes from your heart to your mind and finally to your hands. While drawing inspiration from my travels across the world since a young age I began to understand how women are often treated with so much inequality across many countries. My understanding of inequality was taken to another extreme and that sensation is something that has hugely affected me, both as a person and artist.

My goal is to focus on these women, highlight their shapes, beauty and persona; to keep progressing in the understanding of the realism shapes and take them to a more contemporary feeling and understanding. I want to evoke their aura and I strive for the viewer to appreciate them and their emotions as much as I do.'
